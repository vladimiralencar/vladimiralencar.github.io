Sobre

I am a Ph.D. Professor, Researcher, Writer and Artificial Intelligence Engineer.

I like Coffee, Ecology, History, Geopolitics, Arts, Big Data, AI and Intermittent Fasting.

I have a Ph.D. in Natural Resources with optimization models based on linear programming (2009, UFCG, Brazil). I also hold a Master's Degree in Computer Science (1992, UFCG, Brazil) and two undergraduate degrees: Computer Science (1990) and Radiology (2017).

I work as a Professor and Researcher at Universidade Estadual da Paraíba (UEPB), Campina Grande, Brazil, and I am the Director of the Data Analysis Laboratory (LANA).
